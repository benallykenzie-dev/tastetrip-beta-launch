Delete Account

Request deletion of your TasteTrip account and associated personal data.

How to Request Deletion

TasteTrip users can request account deletion at any time by contacting TasteTrip support from the email address connected to their account.

  1. Email tastetrip.app@gmail.com.
  2. Use the subject line: Delete my TasteTrip account.
  3. Include the email address connected to your TasteTrip account.

We may ask you to confirm that you control the account email before completing the deletion request.

What Will Be Deleted

When your deletion request is completed, TasteTrip will delete or disconnect personal information associated with your account, including:

  • Account profile information, such as name and email address.
  • Saved restaurants and dietary preferences.
  • Notification and location settings.
  • Reviews, beta feedback, support requests, and restaurant reports tied to your account where reasonably possible.

Information We May Keep Briefly

Some records may be retained for a limited time when needed for security, fraud prevention, legal compliance, abuse prevention, backup recovery, or app integrity. These records are kept only as long as reasonably necessary.

Processing Time

TasteTrip will review deletion requests and aim to complete them within 30 days after verifying the request.

Questions about account deletion? Contact us at tastetrip.app@gmail.com.
