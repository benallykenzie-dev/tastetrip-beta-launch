Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 27, 2026

Overview

TasteTrip is a beta restaurant discovery app that helps users find local restaurants, menu items, deals, reviews, dietary information, and local food recommendations. This Privacy Policy explains what information TasteTrip collects, how it is used, and the choices users have.

TasteTrip is currently in beta. Some features may change as the app moves toward its first public version.

Information We Collect

Depending on how you use TasteTrip, we may collect:

  • Account information: name, email address, account ID, sign-in status, and beta access status.
  • Profile settings: dietary preferences, saved restaurants, notification preferences, and location settings.
  • Location information: manually entered location details or device location permission choices, if you choose to enable location features.
  • User-submitted content: restaurant reviews, food item reviews, support requests, beta feedback, restaurant suggestions, and reports about incorrect restaurant or menu information.
  • App activity: actions needed to operate the app, such as saving restaurants, changing preferences, submitting feedback, or viewing restaurant and menu details.
  • Technical information: basic device, browser, app, and diagnostic information used to keep the beta working and improve reliability.

How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Create and manage TasteTrip accounts.
  • Control beta access and waitlist access.
  • Save user preferences, saved restaurants, reviews, and settings.
  • Show restaurant, menu, dietary, and location-based discovery features.
  • Respond to support requests, bug reports, and beta feedback.
  • Improve TasteTrip features, usability, safety, and reliability.
  • Protect against misuse, spam, unauthorized access, or security issues.
  • Comply with legal, safety, and platform requirements.

Location

TasteTrip may use location settings to help users discover nearby restaurants and local recommendations. You can adjust location permissions in your device settings and manage location preferences in the app. You may also use TasteTrip with a manually entered location where available.

Payments and Orders

TasteTrip beta is focused on restaurant discovery. Some prototype checkout, cart, or order screens may appear during beta testing. TasteTrip does not intentionally collect or process real payment card numbers in the beta. If payment features are added in the future, payment information will be handled through a secure payment provider, and this policy will be updated.

How We Share Information

We do not sell personal information. We may share limited information only when needed:

  • With service providers that help operate TasteTrip, such as hosting, authentication, database, maps, and app distribution services.
  • When required by law, regulation, legal process, or platform safety requirements.
  • To protect TasteTrip, users, restaurants, or the public from fraud, abuse, security issues, or harmful activity.
  • With your consent or at your direction.

Service Providers

TasteTrip may use third-party services, including Supabase for authentication and database services, Netlify for web hosting, Google Play for app distribution, and Google Maps or map links for location-related features. These providers process information according to their own privacy practices and our configuration of the app.

Data Retention

We keep information for as long as needed to provide TasteTrip, support beta testing, comply with legal obligations, resolve issues, and improve the app. Users may request deletion of their account or personal information by contacting us or visiting our Delete Account page.

Your Choices

  • You can update notification, dietary, saved restaurant, and location settings in the app.
  • You can turn off device location permissions in your device settings.
  • You can contact us to request access, correction, or deletion of personal information.
  • You can request account deletion on the Delete Account page.
  • You can stop using TasteTrip at any time.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect user information. No online service can guarantee complete security, but we work to keep TasteTrip beta protected as it grows.

Children

TasteTrip is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided information to TasteTrip, please contact us so we can review and delete it if needed.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as TasteTrip changes. If we make meaningful changes, we will update the effective date and make the revised policy available through the app or launch website.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your information, contact TasteTrip at tastetrip.app@gmail.com.
